There Are Three Comman Resume Formats


Chronological

Classic Format that list your work Experience in order , Starting with the most recent

Functional

Emphasizes Qualification and Accomplishments instead of specific jobs. but isn't recommended

Hybrid

Modern formats where skills and highlights go at the top before detailed work history
